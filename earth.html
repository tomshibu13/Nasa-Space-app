Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known place in the universe where life exists. It is a dynamic and diverse world with vast oceans, a protective atmosphere, and a variety of ecosystems that support a wide range of life forms.

  • Size: Earth has a diameter of about 12,742 kilometers (7,918 miles), making it the fifth-largest planet in the solar system.

  • Orbit: Earth orbits the Sun at an average distance of about 150 million kilometers (93 million miles), which is known as 1 astronomical unit (AU). It takes 365.25 days to complete one orbit, leading to a leap year every four years.

  • Temperature:Earth's average surface temperature is about 15°C (59°F), though temperatures vary widely depending on location and time of year.
