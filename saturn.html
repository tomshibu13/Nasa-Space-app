Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the solar system, known for its stunning system of rings that make it one of the most iconic planets. It is a gas giant like Jupiter, but its rings and unique moons set it apart.

  • Size:Saturn has a diameter of about 120,536 kilometers (74,898 miles), making it about nine times wider than Earth.

  • Orbit: Saturn orbits the Sun at an average distance of about 1.43 billion kilometers (888 million miles). One year on Saturn is equivalent to 29.5 Earth years.

  • Rotation: Saturn has a fast rotation, with one day (a rotation on its axis) lasting about 10.7 Earth hours.
